About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at School of Computer and Control Engineering, University of Chinese Academy Sciences, supervised by Prof. Yong Shi. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in Software Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology in June, 2013.

Research Interest

  • Computer Vision: contour detection, multimodal learning
  • Network Analysis: network embedding, community detection
